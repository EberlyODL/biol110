Module 01 Assessment Part 1: Google Docs

To successfully complete this assessment, you will:

  1. Make a copy of this Google Document so that you can edit it.
  2. Type in your answers to the questions in the Questions and Answers section of this document.
  3. Share the document with your TA.
  4. Download the document as a PDF.
  5. Upload the PDF to the ANGEL dropbox for this module.

Module 01 Assessment Part 2: VoiceThread

  1. Create your own VoiceThread that includes:
  • at least one image
  • a comment from you, preferably by webcam or microphone
  • this comment should address a current scientific issue and should take approximately one to two minutes to play back
  • you should introduce the topic and explain its importance.  If there is a controversy - you should explain the controversy and briefly discuss both sides of the controversy.
  1. Share your VoiceThread with your TA by emailing the link of your finished VoiceThread to your TA.
  2. Also copy/paste the link to your VoiceThread in this document.
  3. After entering your VoiceThread link, from the "File" drop down menu, select "Download as..." and choose "PDF."
  4. Submit the PDF (e.g. "Copy of Module 06 Assessment_johnsmith.pdf") to the Module 01 Assessment - Drop Box in ANGEL. See Google Course Calendar for specific due dates. You will submit only ONE document.
