Even though climate change and the media coverage surrounding it can appear overwhelming, there are a number of ways you can take direct action to make your home and lifestyle more environmentally friendly and to reduce your personal carbon footprint. A carbon footprint is a measure of the amount of greenhouse gas (or carbon dioxide equivalent) that is produced from our daily activities over the course of a year.
