Project Requirements

The requirements of the project are that you use the scientific method to:

  1. ask an interesting and topical biological question
  2. generate the appropriate Null and Alternative hypothesis
  3. gather the appropriate information from reliable sources to test your hypotheses
  4. use supporting evidence to provide conclusions on your research findings as well as any future studies or recommendations that you think are important or necessary

Additional Project Requirements:

  • The steps of the scientific method need to be followed in the correct and logical order.
  • The VoiceThread needs to be limited to less than five minutes.
  • The text and graphics need to be of sufficient size and quality to be visible on the VoiceThread format.
  • Use at least five 'good quality' reliable references. View the Guide to Referencing.
  • Follow the Project Tips.

VoiceThread Requirements:

  1. Title - should be catchy yet informative. Also a good place to introduce yourself.
  2. Introduction - provide background information and research question.
  3. Hypothesis - Null and Alternative hypotheses stated.
  4. Data Gathered - akin to research methods whereby the data you gathered is summarized and presented in words, tables, graphs, graphics etc.
  5. Conclusion - whether hypotheses were accepted or rejected, why, and what the data is saying.  Include other interesting findings you uncovered during your research or other relating information.
  6. Reference list

Grading Rubric

The TAs will be using the Module 10 Rubric for Students to grade your VoiceThread projects. The TAs will place a √ or X alongside each point and then give you a grade for each of the 8 different sections highlighted in gray. The composite of these 8 subsections will form the final total score.
