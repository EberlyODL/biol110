Use Copyright-Free images in your VoiceThread: Images are often protected by copyright.  That means you cannot simply Google for images and use what you find.  Instead, you must search for images that photographers have made available for use without permissions or copyright protections.  You can find sources for such images below:
  • Flickr Creative Commons. Images found in Flickr's creative commons gallery can literally be used for almost any project that is related to education with nothing more than a credit to the original photographer. Be sure to read over the photographer's rules before reusing images.
  • Wikimedia. Be sure to read the Reusing Content Outside Wikimedia before reusing any images.
